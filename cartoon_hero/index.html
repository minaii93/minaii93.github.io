About Susie

Susie McCallister is a witch, the head counselor on Summer Camp Island and the main antagonist of the show. The camp is a host to many bizarre occurrences such as horses that transform into unicorns, talking sharks, yetis, haunted cabins, post-it notes that lead to other dimensions, and nosey monsters that live under the bed.

Susie and her friends

Susie's characteristics

  1. She can be mean and condescending to the campers at times but deep down, she does display some responsible and sympathetic behavior.
  2. she is immortal due to drinking an immortality potion. Which means she is 400 years old in a 15 years old body.
  3. She is able to use telekinesis, teleporting, reversing time, making clones and all kind of cool stuff what superheros do.
